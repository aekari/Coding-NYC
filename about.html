Welcome to our website, dedicated to providing information and resources for those interested in learning how to code in NYC. Our mission is to empower individuals to pursue a career in technology by providing guidance and support on their coding journey.

We believe that coding is a valuable skill that can open doors to a variety of exciting career opportunities. Whether you're interested in web development, data science, or software engineering, our website offers a wealth of information on how to get started.

Our team of experienced educators and tech professionals have curated a list of coding bootcamps, online courses, and in-person classes that are available in the NYC area. We also provide resources on coding meetups and events in the city, as well as job placement assistance for those who have completed a coding program.

We understand that learning to code can be a daunting task, which is why we strive to create a welcoming and supportive community. We are passionate about helping individuals discover their potential and achieve their career goals in the exciting world of technology. Thank you for visiting our website and we look forward to supporting you on your coding journey.
